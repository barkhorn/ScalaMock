Issue tracker

ScalaMock issues can be submitted to our issues page on github.

Stack Overflow

You can also ask questions on Stack Overflow with #scalamock tag - we have subscription for questions tagged "scalamock".

Disqus

ScalaMock does not have its own mailing list - we believe mailing lists are too cumbersome to use and can be easily replaced by dedicated software or services: issue trackers for bug reports and feature requests, Stack Overflow for community support and solving specific problems, Twitter for announcements, etc.

Anyway, you can still leave your comment or question using Disqus. Guest posts are allowed and you don't have to enter valid e-mail address. Also no one will see your post until it's accepted.
